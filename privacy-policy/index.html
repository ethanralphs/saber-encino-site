Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sport Clips Haircuts of Encino (store CA705), operator of the website encinomenshaircuts.com ("we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our Site, contact us, or use our online check-in and signup forms. Our Salon is located at 17200 Ventura Blvd, Encino, CA 91316, and is an independently owned and operated Sport Clips franchise location.

We've written this policy in plain language and with California residents in mind. It is intended to be transparent about our practices; it is not a substitute for individualized legal advice.

Information We Collect

We collect information you choose to give us and a limited amount of information collected automatically when you use the Site.

Information you provide. When you fill out a contact form, sign up for offers or text/email alerts, or use online check-in, you may provide:

  • Your name;
  • Your mobile phone number;
  • Your email address; and
  • Any message or preferences you choose to include.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we and our service providers may automatically collect standard technical information such as your IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and how you arrived at the Site. This is collected through cookies and similar technologies (see below).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide the services you request;
  • Manage online check-in and let you know about wait times and availability;
  • Send you offers, promotions, and updates you have signed up to receive;
  • Understand how the Site is used so we can improve it; and
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and the safety of our guests and staff.

If you sign up to receive text messages, we will only use your mobile number to send the messages you opted into. Message and data rates may apply, and you can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Analytics & Marketing Tools

We use third-party tools to operate our marketing, forms, and website analytics. In particular, we use GoHighLevel (a customer engagement and marketing platform) to manage contact form submissions, signups, and communications, and we may use standard web analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand Site traffic. These providers process information on our behalf and may set their own cookies. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the independent privacy practices of these providers, and we encourage you to review their policies.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We and our service providers use cookies and similar technologies to keep the Site working properly, remember preferences, and measure how the Site performs. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the Site function.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these limited situations:

  • With service providers (such as GoHighLevel and analytics providers) who process information on our behalf to help us operate the Site and communicate with you;
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of our guests, staff, or the public; and
  • In connection with a business transfer, such as a sale of the Salon, in which case information would remain subject to a comparable privacy commitment.

Your Choices & Privacy Rights

You are in control of the information you share with us:

  • Marketing messages. You can unsubscribe from emails using the link in any message, or stop text messages by replying STOP.
  • Access, correction & deletion. You may ask us to review, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you.

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to request deletion, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. Because we do not sell your personal information, no opt-out of "sale" is necessary. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below; we will verify your request before acting on it, and we will not treat you differently for making one.

Data Retention & Security

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy or as required by law, and we use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect it. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, however, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

The Site is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. While we happily provide haircuts for kids, any information related to a minor should be submitted by a parent or guardian.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
