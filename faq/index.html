FAQ

Good to know
before you come in.

Do I need an appointment?+

No — walk-ins are always welcome. To skip the wait, check in online before you head over and we'll have a chair ready.

How much does a haircut cost?+

A men's haircut is $32. Adding the MVP Experience (hot towel, shampoo, neck & shoulder treatment) is $38. Kids' and senior cuts are $30. Beard trims are $15.

Do you take walk-ins during busy times?+

Yes, always. Checking in online ahead of time is the best way to minimize your wait during peak hours.

What's included in a men's haircut?+

Every men's cut includes a consultation, precision cut, style finish, and a free ear and neck trim.

Can I request a specific Stylist?+

Yes — once you've visited a few times, you can request your preferred Stylist when you check in online.

Do you sell hair products?+

Yes, we carry a full retail lineup of styling products your Stylist can recommend based on your hair type and style.

Is parking available?+

Yes, free parking is available right outside the shop.

What are your hours?+

Monday through Friday 10am–7pm, Saturday 9am–6pm, and Sunday 10am–6pm.

Do you cut kids' hair?+

Yes — kids' haircuts are $30 and we take extra time with first-time and nervous clients.

What is the MVP Experience?+

It's our upgraded haircut experience — a precision cut plus a massaging shampoo, hot steamed towel, and a neck & shoulder treatment, for $38.

Do you offer beard trims?+

Yes, beard trims are $15 and can be added to any haircut or booked on their own.

Is there a first-visit offer for new clients?+

Yes — new clients get a free MVP upgrade on their first visit. Sign up here to get it sent to your phone.
